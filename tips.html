Travel Tips

Essential tips for making the most of your trip to Japan.

Key Tips

  • Learn Basic Japanese Phrases: Knowing a few key phrases can enhance your experience and help you connect with locals.
  • Cash is King: Credit cards are not widely accepted; carry cash and use ATMs in convenience stores.
  • Etiquette Matters: Respect local customs, such as bowing when greeting and removing shoes indoors.
  • Public Transport Tips: Use Japan's efficient public transport; consider getting a Japan Rail Pass for intercity travel.
  • Stay Connected: Rent a portable WiFi device for easy internet access during your trip.
  • Try Local Foods: Don't miss out on regional specialties; exploring local cuisine is a big part of the experience.
  • Plan Your Itinerary: Research attractions and transport options to make the most of your time.
  • Be Polite: Politeness is key in Japanese culture; a simple "thank you" (arigato) goes a long way.
